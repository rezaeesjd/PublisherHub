# Brief — Best Day Trips from Milan (TOFU Destination Guide)

## Purpose
TOFU discovery asset for the `cinque-terre-from-milan` cluster. Captures early-stage search ("what can I do as a day trip from Milan?") and routes that traveller progressively toward the Cinque Terre BOFU booking page via the MOFU comparison post.

## Target reader
A first-time visitor to Milan, or someone planning a Milan-anchored trip, who is still in the discovery phase. They are deciding *where* to go for a day, not yet *how* to book a specific tour. They are open to Cinque Terre, the lakes, or the Swiss Alps and want a single, honest overview.

## CTA target
Primary: [Book the Cinque Terre full-day tour from Milan](https://www.milano-adventures.com/tours/cinque-terre-full-day-tour-from-milan-riomaggiore-manarola/) (the headline option of this guide).
Secondary: cluster cross-links to the Lake Como + Lugano and Bernina + St Moritz sibling clusters where their public pages are live.

## Cluster role
TOFU destination guide → links to MOFU comparison post (`cinque-terre-full-day-tour-from-milan-vs-diy`) and BOFU main booking post (`cinque-terre-full-day-tour-from-milan`).

## Key topics covered
1. Why Milan is a strong day-trip base (rail connectivity, central position).
2. Cinque Terre as the headline coastal day trip (five villages, ferry, full-day timing).
3. Lake Como + Lugano as the lakes + Switzerland-in-a-day alternative.
4. Bernina Express + St Moritz as the scenic-rail Alpine alternative.
5. How to choose: time of year, energy level, scenery preference.
6. Honest logistics framing (long days, early starts, weather dependencies).

## Tone
Discovery-stage, honest, no hype. Helps the reader pick a destination rather than pressuring a booking.
