# FAQ

## What is the best day trip from Milan?
The most popular single day trip from Milan is Cinque Terre — five UNESCO-listed Ligurian fishing villages visited in one structured day. Lake Como + Lugano and the Bernina Express + St Moritz are the strongest alternatives if you want lakes or alpine scenery instead.

## How long does a day trip from Milan take?
Full-day trips from Milan typically run 10 to 14 hours door-to-door. The Milano Adventures Cinque Terre tour is approximately 13 hours 30 minutes, with a 07:00 departure from Piazza IV Novembre.

## Can you do Cinque Terre as a day trip from Milan?
Yes — and it's the most-requested day trip from the city. The Milano Adventures full-day tour covers all five villages (Riomaggiore, Manarola, Corniglia, Vernazza, Monterosso al Mare) on Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday, leaving at 07:00.

## Do I need a passport for the day trips that cross into Switzerland?
The Lake Como + Lugano trip and the Bernina Express + St Moritz trip both cross into Switzerland. Bring your passport for any border crossing, even when it's informal under Schengen rules, and confirm border policy with your operator at booking.

## Which day trip is best for first-time visitors to Italy?
Cinque Terre. It delivers the most iconic Italian coastal scenery in a single day and is the simplest to book as a guided full-day package.

## When is the best time of year for these day trips?
Cinque Terre is at its best from spring through autumn (warmer sea, more reliable ferry access). Lake Como is mildest April through October. The Bernina Express runs year-round — winter for snow scenery, summer for clearer mountain views.

## Are guided day trips from Milan worth it?
For most travellers, yes. Guided full-day tours bundle the rail and coach logistics, the inter-village or border-crossing connections, and (for Cinque Terre) the seasonal ferry — all of which take real planning to do independently in a single day.

## What is the cancellation policy on Milano Adventures day trips?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure. Within 24 hours, the booking is non-refundable. Always confirm the current terms on the booking page.
