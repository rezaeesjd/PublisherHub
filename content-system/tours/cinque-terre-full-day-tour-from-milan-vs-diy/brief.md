# Brief — Cinque Terre MOFU Comparison Post

## Purpose
MOFU decision-support page for the cinque-terre-from-milan cluster. Addresses the "guided vs DIY" hesitation for travellers who want to see Cinque Terre from Milan but are undecided on how. The post validates both options honestly — DIY is possible and worth considering — but routes travellers who choose guided to the BOFU booking page.

## Target reader
Traveller who wants to do Cinque Terre from Milan and is genuinely comparing their options. May have already seen the BOFU booking post and is now evaluating whether the guided tour is worth the price over a self-planned train day.

## CTA target
[Book on Milano Adventures](https://www.milano-adventures.com/tours/cinque-terre-full-day-tour-from-milan-riomaggiore-manarola/)

## Cluster role
MOFU comparison post → links to BOFU main booking post (`cinque-terre-full-day-tour-from-milan`).

## Key decision factors addressed
1. Honest cost comparison (DIY is not automatically cheaper)
2. Ferry access — same weather-dependency for both options
3. Which villages are covered and in what format
4. Planning complexity of the DIY option from Milan
5. Who each option genuinely suits
