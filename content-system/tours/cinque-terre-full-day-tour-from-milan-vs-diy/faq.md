# FAQ

## Is DIY Cinque Terre from Milan cheaper than a guided tour?
Not necessarily. The guided tour price covers all transport. DIY requires separate rail tickets, a Cinque Terre card for trail/train access, and any ferry purchase — the combined cost can equal or exceed the tour price.

## Does the ferry run on both the guided tour and DIY?
Yes and no — the same way. The ferry is seasonal and weather-dependent for all travellers. Neither option guarantees ferry access; it operates when conditions allow.

## Does the guided tour cover all five villages?
Yes — Riomaggiore, Manarola, Corniglia, Vernazza, and Monterosso al Mare.

## Which days does the guided tour run?
Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday.

## How many people are on the guided tour?
Maximum 22.

## What languages does the guided tour guide speak?
English and Spanish.

## Can I do Cinque Terre from Milan in one day by myself?
Yes, it is feasible but requires planning: the rail connection from Milan to Cinque Terre, inter-village transport, and the return all add up to a long day with careful timing.
