# FAQ

## How long is this tour?
Approximately 10 to 11 hours, departing Milan at 08:30.

## Where is the meeting point in Milan?
Milan Visitor Centre at Largo Cairoli 18, on the corner of Via Cusani and Largo Cairoli (metro MM1 Cairoli / MM2 Lanza).

## Which days does the tour operate?
Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, and Saturdays per the source schedule.

## Is the Lake Como cruise included?
Yes — a roughly one-hour sightseeing boat cruise across Lake Como is included.

## How much time will I have in Como and Lugano?
The source lists about three hours of free time in Como's historic centre and about three hours in Lugano.

## Does this tour really cross into Switzerland?
Yes. The afternoon is spent in Lugano in the Canton of Ticino, Switzerland, before returning to Milan.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet at the designated Milan Visitor Centre meeting point.

## What languages are offered?
English is listed as the guide and service language.

## Is the tour wheelchair or stroller accessible?
The source marks wheelchair, stroller, and service-animal access as not available.
