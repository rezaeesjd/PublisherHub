# Brief

- Funnel: MOFU
- Audience: Milan-based travelers comparing two-lake day trips and deciding whether to book a guided option or DIY by train.

- Variant differentiator vs. the All-in-One Como/Bellagio/Lugano BOFU asset: tighter routing, more free time in Como historic centre and Lugano, fewer stops.
- Brand voice: friendly, practical, trust-building.
