# Pisa Afternoon Tour from Florence: Skip-the-Line Leaning Tower in Half a Day

If you're staying in Florence and want to see the Leaning Tower of Pisa without losing a full day, this afternoon tour with **Milano Adventures** is one of the easiest ways to do it. You meet near Florence Central Station, travel to Pisa, and visit the Piazza dei Miracoli with a guide — including priority access to the Leaning Tower itself.

## Why travelers choose this tour
- Half-day format (about 5 hours 30 minutes) that leaves your morning free in Florence
- Guided tour of Piazza dei Miracoli, a UNESCO World Heritage Site
- Priority access to the Leaning Tower of Pisa
- Round-trip transport from Florence — no train logistics to plan
- Time inside the Pisa Cathedral, plus the Baptistery and Monumental Graveyard

## What to expect
You'll meet at Piazzale Montelungo, a 5–10 minute walk from Florence Central Station, where the guide departs at 14:15. After a short drive through the Tuscan countryside, you'll arrive in Pisa and walk through the medieval entrance gate to the Piazza dei Miracoli.

The guided portion covers the Cathedral interior, the Baptistery and Monumental Graveyard, and the Leaning Tower with priority entry. You'll also have free time in Pisa before the return transfer back to Florence.

## Is this tour right for you?
This experience suits travelers who want a structured, time-efficient way to see Pisa with a guide rather than navigating trains and ticket queues themselves. Note that children under 8 are not permitted to climb the Leaning Tower, and the tour is not suitable for guests using wheelchairs or with impaired mobility. Comfortable walking shoes are recommended.
