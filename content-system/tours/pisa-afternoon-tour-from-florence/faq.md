# FAQ

## How long is this tour?
The experience is listed as approximately 5 hours and 30 minutes.

## Where is the meeting point in Florence?
At Piazzale Montelungo, Firenze FI, Italy — within a 5–10 minute walk from Florence Central Station. Plan to arrive 15 minutes early; the team wears fuchsia shirts.

## What time does the tour depart?
Departure is at 14:15 from the Tours & News Kiosk.

## Is the Leaning Tower climb included?
Yes — Leaning Tower entrance with priority access is included. Children under 8 are not permitted to climb the Leaning Tower.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet at the designated meeting point in Florence.

## Is this tour wheelchair accessible?
No. The source notes this tour is not suitable for guests with wheelchairs or impaired mobility. Service animals are also not permitted.

## Which languages are listed?
English and Spanish are listed in the source details.
