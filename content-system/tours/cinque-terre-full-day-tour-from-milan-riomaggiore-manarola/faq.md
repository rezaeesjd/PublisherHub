# FAQ — Cinque Terre Full-Day Tour from Milan

## Where and when does the tour start?
Departure is at 07:00 from Piazza IV Novembre in front of Hotel Gallia, near Milano Centrale station. Please arrive 15 minutes early. Look for staff in fuchsia shirts.

## Which villages does the tour cover?
All five Cinque Terre villages: Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore.

## How long is the day?
Total duration is 13 hours 30 minutes door-to-door from Milan, including roughly 2.5 hours of road time each way.

## What days does it run?
Mondays, Wednesdays, and Saturdays.

## What languages are offered?
The tour is led by an expert tour leader in English and Spanish.

## How is the transport organized between villages?
A GT coach takes you from Milan to the Ligurian coast, then pre-booked regional trains link the villages. A panoramic ferry segment may be included when sea and weather conditions allow; otherwise the train is used.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet the group at Piazza IV Novembre in Milan.

## How big is the group?
The maximum group size is 22 travelers.

## How difficult is the walking?
Difficulty is rated easy. Expect short, paced walks within each village. Comfortable shoes are recommended.

## What does it cost?
Adults (12–99): €275. Children (4–11): €157. Infants (0–3): free.

## How is the booking confirmed?
Confirmation is instant. Tickets are accepted on paper or mobile, one per booking.

## What about the cancellation policy?
For the latest cancellation terms, please check the live booking page before you reserve.

## Can I book on TripAdvisor instead of Viator?
Yes — the tour is also listed on TripAdvisor. Viator is the primary booking channel; TripAdvisor is shown as a secondary option.
