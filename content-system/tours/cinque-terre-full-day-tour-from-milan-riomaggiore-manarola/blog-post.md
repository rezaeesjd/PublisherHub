## Why this tour is worth a full day from Milan
Cinque Terre is a UNESCO World Heritage Site stitched into the cliffs of South Liguria near La Spezia. From Milan it is a 2.5-hour coach ride each way — long enough that the logistics matter, short enough that you can still walk the harbor in Vernazza, taste pesto in Monterosso, and watch the lights come on over Riomaggiore in the same day. With an expert tour leader, a comfortable air-conditioned coach, and pre-booked train tickets between villages, the day flows without you having to think about transfers.

## Who this tour is best for
- Travelers based in Milan who want to see the real Cinque Terre without renting a car or solving the regional rail map
- First-time visitors who want all five villages, not just one or two highlights
- Couples and small groups who want a guided full-day with breathing room (group cap is 22)
- Easy-paced walkers — the difficulty is rated easy, with manageable strolls in each village
