# FAQ

## Is a passport required for both options?
Yes. The Bernina Express crosses from Italy into Switzerland regardless of how you travel. A valid passport is required whether you join a guided tour or plan independently.

## What does the guided tour price include?
The guided tour price (from EUR 113.85) includes roundtrip GT coach transport between Milan and Tirano, a 2nd-class Bernina Express ticket with opening windows, and an English-speaking tour leader for the full day.

## Does the guided tour depart every day?
Yes — the guided tour operates daily, Monday through Sunday.

## Can I do the Bernina Express from Milan in one day without a tour?
Yes, but it requires planning: getting to Tirano from Milan (approx. 2 hours), booking Bernina Express tickets in advance (especially in high season), and managing returns. Total logistics time is significant.

## What is the guided tour cancellation policy?
All bookings are non-refundable — all sales final. Confirm your dates before booking.

## How big is the guided tour group?
Maximum 20 travellers.

## Which option is cheaper?
This depends on how you calculate DIY costs. The guided tour price covers coach + Bernina Express + tour leader in one amount. DIY requires separate tickets for the Milan–Tirano leg and the Bernina Express, plus any St Moritz or alpine stop costs. The total can be comparable or higher.
