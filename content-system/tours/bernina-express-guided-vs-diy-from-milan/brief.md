# Brief — Bernina MOFU Comparison Post

## Purpose
MOFU decision-support page for the swiss-alps-from-milan cluster. Addresses the "guided vs DIY" hesitation that blocks travellers who are sold on the Bernina Express experience but undecided on how to do it. The post validates both options honestly and routes travellers who choose guided to the BOFU booking page.

## Target reader
Traveller who knows they want to do the Bernina Express from Milan but is weighing whether to plan independently or join a tour. Likely comparing logistics, cost, and convenience before committing.

## CTA target
[Book on Milano Adventures](https://www.milano-adventures.com/tours/full-day-tour-in-bernina-red-train-and-st-moritz-from-milan/)

## Cluster role
MOFU comparison post → links to BOFU main booking post (`bernina-red-train-and-st-moritz-from-milan`).

## Key decision factors addressed
1. What is actually included in the guided tour price
2. What DIY planning looks like (Milan → Tirano → Bernina Express → St Moritz)
3. Passport requirement (applies both ways — neutralises a DIY advantage)
4. Cost comparison framing (DIY is not necessarily cheaper)
5. Who each option is genuinely best for
