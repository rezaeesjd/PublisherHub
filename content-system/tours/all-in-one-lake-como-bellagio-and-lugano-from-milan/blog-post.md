# Lake Como, Bellagio & Lugano from Milan: One Day, Two Countries

If you only have a single day out of Milan, this all-in-one trip with **Milano Adventures** packs in three of the region's most photographed destinations — Como, Bellagio, and Lugano — plus a scenic ferry crossing on Lake Como.

## Why travelers choose this day trip
- Three destinations in one full day from Milan
- Scenic Lake Como ferry crossing toward Bellagio
- Free time in each town for lunch, photos, or shopping
- Cross into Switzerland for Lugano without the train logistics
- Professional tour leader and earphones for live commentary

## What to expect
You'll meet at Piazza IV Novembre near Milano Centrale at 08:00 and travel by air-conditioned GT coach toward the lakes. The route covers Como (with time for the Duomo and lakeside promenade), a panoramic ferry segment via Argegno, free time in Bellagio, and an afternoon in Lugano before returning to Milan.

The itinerary order may shift for logistical reasons, but the experience is built around a 10–11 hour day.

## Is this tour right for you?
Best for travelers who want one structured day to see the highlights of Lake Como and a Swiss lakeside city without planning multiple connections themselves. Hotel pickup is not included; travelers meet at the central meeting point in Milan.
