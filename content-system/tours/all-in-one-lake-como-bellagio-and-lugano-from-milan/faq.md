# FAQ

## How long is this tour?
Approximately 10 to 11 hours, departing Milan at 08:00.

## Where is the meeting point in Milan?
Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia near Milano Centrale.

## Does the tour really visit Switzerland?
Yes. The afternoon includes Lugano in Canton Ticino, Switzerland, before the return drive to Milan.

## Is the Lake Como cruise included?
Yes — a panoramic ferry crossing on Lake Como is included as part of the routing.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet at the designated meeting point.

## What languages are listed?
English and Spanish.

## Will the order of stops always be the same?
The source notes the order may be adjusted for logistical reasons.
