## Route highlights

Your day combines cross-border Alpine scenery with structured stops:

1. **Tirano** for the transition to the rail segment.
2. **Bernina scenic crossing** on one of Europe’s most memorable mountain routes.
3. **Alp Grüm** panoramic stop.
4. **Bernina Diavolezza** glacier-view area.
5. **Pontresina** village stop.
6. **St Moritz** free time to explore.
7. **Lake St Moritz** area.
8. **Bernina return segment** and transfer back to Milan.
