# FAQ — Cinque Terre Day Trip from Milan (Variant v2)

### Can you really do Cinque Terre as a day trip from Milan?
Yes. The coach reaches the Ligurian coast in about 150 minutes each way, and we use pre-booked regional trains (and a seasonal ferry when conditions allow) to connect all five villages — Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore — inside one day. The full day runs about 13 hours 30 minutes.

### What time does the tour leave Milan?
07:00 from Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia near Milano Centrale. Look for our team in fuchsia shirts and please arrive 15 minutes early.

### Which days does the tour run?
Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday.

### How much does it cost?
Adult €275, child (4–11) €157, infant (0–3) free. Pricing is per person in EUR. Confirmation is instant when you book.

### Do we visit all five Cinque Terre villages?
Yes — Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore are all on the itinerary, with the supplier-provided panoramic ferry segment included when sea and weather conditions allow.

### Is hotel pickup included?
No. This is a meeting-point tour: travelers meet at Piazza IV Novembre at 07:00.

### What languages does the guide speak?
Live commentary is provided in English and Spanish by an official expert tour leader.

### How many people are in the group?
A maximum of 22 travelers per departure.

### Is the tour wheelchair accessible?
Wheelchair accessibility was not confirmed by the supplier for this tour. Please contact us before booking if accessibility is essential.

### What if the ferry doesn't run on the day?
The panoramic ferry segment is seasonal and weather-dependent. On certain days — especially outside peak season — the ferry may not operate, and your guide will switch the leg to the train. The rest of the day continues as planned.

### What's the cancellation policy?
Cancellation specifics depend on the booking channel. Please review the cancellation policy on the booking page before you reserve.
