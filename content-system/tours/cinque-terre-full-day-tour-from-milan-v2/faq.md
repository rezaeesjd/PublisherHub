# FAQ

## Is this really possible as a day trip from Milan?
Yes. This tour is designed as a long full-day itinerary (about 13 hours 30 minutes) with organized transport.

## Where does the tour start?
At Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia near Milano Centrale station.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet at the designated meeting point.

## Which villages are covered?
The supplier description references the core Cinque Terre villages, including Riomaggiore, Manarola, Monterosso, Vernazza, and Corniglia.

## Is the ferry always included?
Ferry operation is seasonal and weather-dependent. Alternative routing may be used when ferry service is unavailable.

## What languages is this tour offered in?
English and Spanish.
