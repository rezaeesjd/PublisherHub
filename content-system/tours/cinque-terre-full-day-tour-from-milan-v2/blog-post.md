# Cinque Terre Day Trip from Milan (Five Villages)

Looking for a simple way to visit all five Cinque Terre villages in one day? This guided day trip from Milan helps you do exactly that—without managing the train and ferry logistics on your own—with support from Milano Adventures.

You’ll depart from **Piazza IV Novembre (Hotel Gallia bus stop), Milan**, then travel toward the Ligurian coast by comfortable coach. Once in the Cinque Terre area, your day combines guided support and free time to enjoy iconic villages such as **Riomaggiore, Manarola, Monterosso, Vernazza, and Corniglia**.

## Why travelers choose this day trip

- Round-trip transport from Milan included
- Multi-stop Cinque Terre village experience in one day
- English and Spanish tour operation
- Seasonal panoramic ferry segment when sea/weather conditions allow
- Max group size: 22 travelers

## Important practical details

- **Duration:** approximately 13 hours 30 minutes
- **Departure time:** 07:00 (Mon, Wed, Sat)
- **Meeting point:** Piazza IV Novembre, 20124 Milano MI, Italy (in front of Hotel Gallia)
- **Check-in recommendation:** arrive at least 15 minutes early
- **Hotel pickup/drop-off:** not included
