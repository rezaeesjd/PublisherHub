# Brief — Cinque Terre FAQ Support Post

## Purpose
FAQ objection-removal page for the cinque-terre-from-milan cluster. Removes practical barriers to booking by answering the highest-friction questions: ferry reliability, what's included, group size, departure logistics, and how to book directly. Routes confident readers to the BOFU booking page.

## Target reader
Traveller who has already decided they want to do Cinque Terre from Milan and has a specific practical question (especially about the ferry or what "included" actually means) that is blocking their booking decision.

## CTA target
[Book on Milano Adventures](https://www.milano-adventures.com/tours/cinque-terre-full-day-tour-from-milan-riomaggiore-manarola/)

## Cluster role
FAQ support post → links to BOFU main booking post (`cinque-terre-full-day-tour-from-milan`).

## Key objections addressed
1. Ferry uncertainty (most common objection for this tour)
2. Group size / crowd concern
3. Meeting point / logistics clarity
4. Language of the guide
5. Which days the tour runs
6. Direct booking preference
