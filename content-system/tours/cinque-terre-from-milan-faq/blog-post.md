# Cinque Terre from Milan: Your Questions Answered Before You Book

A full-day trip to Cinque Terre from Milan is one of the most requested day tours in northern Italy — and it also comes with the most pre-booking questions. Below you'll find straight answers on the practical details that matter: ferry reliability, what's actually included in the price, where to meet, and what the group size looks like in practice.

## How long is the tour?

The total duration is approximately 13 hours 30 minutes. It is a full day out — plan to have nothing scheduled back in Milan in the evening.

## What time does it depart and where do we meet?

Departure is at 07:00. The meeting point is Piazza IV Novembre in Milan, in front of the Hotel Gallia bus stop. Arrive at least 15 minutes early. The tour returns to the same location at the end of the day.

## Will the ferry definitely run?

The panoramic ferry connecting the Cinque Terre villages is seasonal and weather-dependent. If sea conditions are poor on the day, the ferry segment may not operate. This is a confirmed fact from the operator — not a worst-case scenario. The rest of the tour continues regardless; the villages are also linked by train.

## What is included in the price?

Your tour covers an expert English- and Spanish-speaking tour leader, land transport between Milan and the Ligurian coast, train travel between the five villages, and — when conditions allow — the panoramic ferry. Hotel pickup and drop-off are not included.

## Is this a small group or a large bus tour?

The group is capped at 22 travellers. This keeps village stops and train boarding manageable without the pressure of a large coach crowd.

## Which five villages does the tour visit?

The full route covers all five Cinque Terre towns: Riomaggiore, Manarola, Corniglia, Vernazza, and Monterosso al Mare. Coverage varies slightly by route and season; the tour leader manages the sequence on the day.

## What languages does the guide speak?

The tour is operated in English and Spanish.

## Which days does the tour run?

Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday. If your travel dates don't align with these days, check the booking page — schedules can be updated seasonally.

## What is the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before departure. This policy applies across all Milano Adventures tours. If you cancel within 24 hours of departure, the booking is non-refundable.
