# FAQ

## How long is the Cinque Terre tour from Milan?
Approximately 13 hours 30 minutes — a full day.

## What time does the tour depart and where?
07:00 from Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia bus stop. Arrive 15 minutes early.

## Will the ferry definitely run?
Not guaranteed. The panoramic ferry is seasonal and weather-dependent. If sea conditions are poor, the ferry is skipped and villages are accessed by train instead.

## What is included?
Expert tour leader (English and Spanish); land transport; inter-village train travel; seasonal panoramic ferry when conditions allow. Hotel pickup/drop-off is not included.

## How many travellers are in the group?
Maximum 22.

## Which villages are visited?
All five: Riomaggiore, Manarola, Corniglia, Vernazza, Monterosso al Mare.

## Which days does the tour run?
Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday.

## What languages does the guide speak?
English and Spanish.

## Can I book directly with the operator?
Yes — direct booking via Milano Adventures is the primary option. Viator and TripAdvisor listings are also available.
