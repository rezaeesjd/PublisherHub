# FAQ

## Is a passport required?
Yes. The tour crosses the Italian-Swiss border. A full valid passport is required for every traveller.

## What is the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable.

## What time does the tour depart?
07:15 from Largo Cairoli 18, Milan (MM1 Cairoli / MM2 Lanza). Look for fuchsia-shirted staff.

## Can I book the day before?
Bookings made less than 12 hours before departure cannot be guaranteed. Provide a valid email and active phone number when booking close to the deadline.

## Is the tour accessible for wheelchair users?
No. The tour is not suitable for guests who use wheelchairs or have impaired mobility.

## What does the ticket price include?
2nd-class Bernina Express ticket (opening windows), roundtrip GT coach from Milan, and an English-speaking expert tour leader for the full day.

## How long is the tour?
Approximately 12 to 13 hours.

## Does the tour run every day?
Yes, daily Monday through Sunday.

## Can children join?
Yes. Price bands cover adult (12–50), child (4–11), and infant (0–3). Infant seats are available.
