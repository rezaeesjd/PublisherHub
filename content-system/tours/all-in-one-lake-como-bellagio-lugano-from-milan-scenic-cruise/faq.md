# FAQ – All-in-One Lake Como, Bellagio & Lugano from Milan + Scenic Cruise

## 1) How long is this tour?
The experience lasts approximately 10 to 11 hours, including travel time and stopovers.

## 2) Where does the tour start and end?
The meeting point is Piazza IV Novembre (Milano Centrale area), in front of Hotel Gallia. The tour ends back at the same meeting point.

## 3) Is transport included?
Yes. Roundtrip transportation by GT coach or minivan is included.

## 4) Is the Lake Como cruise included in the price?
Yes. The panoramic ferry cruise on Lake Como is included.

## 5) How much free time do we get?
You’ll have free time in Como, Bellagio, and Lugano, allowing independent exploration at each destination.

## 6) Is this tour suitable for families with infants?
Most travelers can participate, but infants must sit on laps.

## 7) Is the tour wheelchair accessible?
No, the experience is listed as not wheelchair accessible.

## 8) Can I cancel if my plans change?
Yes. You can cancel up to 24 hours before the experience starts for a full refund.

## 9) Does the itinerary always run in the same order?
Not always. For logistical reasons, the order of stops may change.
