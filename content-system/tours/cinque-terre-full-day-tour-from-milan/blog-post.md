# Cinque Terre Full-Day Tour from Milan

If you want to explore the Ligurian coast without planning trains and transfers on your own, this full-day Cinque Terre experience from Milan offers a structured way to visit the colorful villages in one long but rewarding day.

With Milano Adventures, you’ll start early in Milan and travel by comfortable air-conditioned coach before continuing across Cinque Terre by local rail and (seasonally) ferry connections. The route is designed to help you experience the atmosphere of the five-village coastline efficiently.

## Key details at a glance
- Duration: approximately 13 hours 30 minutes
- Departure city: Milan
- Start time: 07:00 (Mon, Wed, Sat)
- Group size: up to 22 travelers
- Guide languages: English and Spanish
- Transport mix: coach/minibus + train; ferry ride may operate seasonally

## Meeting point
Meet your guide at **Piazza IV Novembre, 20124 Milano MI, Italy**, in front of Hotel Gallia (designated bus stop). Arrival is requested 15 minutes before departure.

## Why travelers choose this format
- Round-trip logistics from Milan are handled for you
- Multi-stop itinerary across Cinque Terre villages
- Small-group cap compared with larger mass-market formats
- Seasonal panoramic ferry segment when sea/weather conditions allow
