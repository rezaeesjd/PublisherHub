# Cinque Terre Full-Day Tour from Milan: All Five Villages in One Day

There's nothing quite like rounding the corner of a Ligurian hillside and watching the pastel-painted cliffside villages of Cinque Terre stack up against the sea below. This guided full-day tour from Milano Adventures takes you from Milan all the way to all five villages — Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore — with roundtrip coach transport, train tickets, and a seasonal panoramic ferry ride all included.

## Why this tour is worth a full day

Cinque Terre is a UNESCO World Heritage Site for good reason. But getting there and between the villages independently — buying the right rail pass, reading train schedules, timing ferry crossings — takes real planning. This tour handles all of it, so you spend your energy on the views, the food, and the scenery rather than the logistics.

### What's included

- **Roundtrip coach transport:** Air-conditioned GT coach from Milan to the Ligurian coast and back
- **Train tickets:** Between villages along the Cinque Terre line
- **Seasonal panoramic ferry:** A boat ride along the coastline between villages (subject to sea and weather — see note below)
- **Expert bilingual guide:** Live commentary in English and Spanish throughout the day
- **All five villages:** Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore

### Who this tour is best for

- First-time visitors to Cinque Terre who want everything arranged in one booking
- Couples and families traveling from Milan who don't want to navigate Italian train logistics independently
- Travelers who want knowledgeable guided storytelling alongside free time to explore each village
- Anyone who wants to see all five villages efficiently and comfortably in a single day

- **Meeting point:** Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia bus stop (Milano Centrale area). Your guide will be in a fuchsia shirt — arrive 15 minutes before departure.
