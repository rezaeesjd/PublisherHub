# Cinque Terre Full-Day Tour from Milan: Visit All Five Villages in One Day

If you’re based in Milan and want a stress-free way to see the Ligurian coast, this guided day trip with **Milano Adventures** gives you a full Cinque Terre experience in a single day.

You’ll depart early from Milano Centrale (Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia), travel by comfortable coach toward La Spezia, and continue with local rail/ferry connections to explore the famous seaside villages: Riomaggiore, Manarola, Corniglia, Vernazza, and Monterosso al Mare.

## Why travelers choose this day trip
- Round-trip transport from Milan is organized for you
- Guided support in English and Spanish
- Seasonal panoramic ferry segment (weather and sea conditions permitting)
- Full-day format with approximately 13 hours 30 minutes total duration
- Ideal for travelers who want scenery, culture, and free time in colorful coastal towns

## Departure details from Milan
Meet your guide at **Piazza IV Novembre, 20124 Milano MI**, at the bus stop in front of Hotel Gallia. Please arrive 15 minutes before departure.

- Departure time: **07:00**
- Operating days: **Monday, Wednesday, Saturday**
- Return point: same as departure meeting point
