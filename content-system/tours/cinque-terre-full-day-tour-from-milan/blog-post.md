# Cinque Terre Full-Day Tour from Milan

## Main Product Content
- Full-day guided day trip from Milan to Cinque Terre.
- Main stops: Riomaggiore, Manarola, and Monterosso al Mare.
- Transport includes GT coach/minivan plus local train; ferry segment is seasonal.
- Includes local train day pass and tour leader support.
- Meeting point: Piazza IV Novembre, Milano (Hotel Gallia bus stop area).
- Duration: about 13 hours 30 minutes.
- Languages: English and Spanish.
- Group format (not private), up to 22 participants.
