# Tour Brief

## Tour title
Cinque Terre Full-Day Tour from Milan: Riomaggiore, Manarola & Monterosso

## Brand
Milano Adventures

## Product reference code
108

## Core offer
A shared full-day guided tour from Milan to Cinque Terre with travel by coach or minivan, local train access, free time in Riomaggiore, Manarola, and Monterosso, and a limoncino or limoncello tasting.

## Traveler intent
This product targets travelers who are already considering a day trip from Milan and want an easy, organized way to experience Cinque Terre without planning transport on their own.

## Funnel stage
Primarily BOFU, with strong MOFU support.

## Conversion goal
Drive direct bookings on the website first using `{{WebsiteLink}}`, while using TripAdvisor and Viator only as secondary trust or alternate discovery references through `{{TripAdvisorLink}}` and `{{ViatorLink}}`.

## Positioning
This experience should be positioned as a convenient, full-day Cinque Terre day trip from Milan for travelers who want scenic coastal villages, guided support, and smoother logistics in a single booking.

## What makes this product compelling
- Departures from Milan meeting point at Piazza IV Novembre
- Full-day format with approximately 13 hours 30 minutes total duration
- Guided shared experience with official guides
- Includes Cinque Terre Card for local train access
- Includes free time in Riomaggiore, Manarola, and Monterosso
- Includes limoncino or limoncello tasting
- Includes access to Cinque Terre National Park
- English and Spanish language support across in-person, audio, and written formats

## Audience fit
Best for:
- visitors staying in Milan who want a long but organized coastal day trip
- travelers who prefer guided logistics instead of self-planning
- couples, friends, and small groups looking for a scenic shared tour
- travelers comfortable with a full-day schedule and moderate walking

Not ideal for:
- travelers needing wheelchair accessibility
- travelers seeking hotel pickup and drop-off
- travelers wanting a private experience

## Content angle
Focus on ease, scenery, and confidence in booking. Emphasize that this is a practical way to visit Cinque Terre from Milan with transport coordination included.

## Key operational facts to preserve
- Meeting point only; no custom pickup
- End point is the same as start point
- Shared group experience, max 22 travelers
- Ferry ride is seasonal and depends on weather and sea conditions
- Itinerary may vary in bad weather
- Comfortable shoes are suggested
- Most travelers can participate
- Infants must sit on laps

## Assumptions
- The main SEO angle should center on "Cinque Terre tour from Milan" and related booking-intent searches
- The website booking page, TripAdvisor page, and Viator page will be inserted dynamically using placeholders
- This content is intended for CMS-ready publishing with minimal manual edits
