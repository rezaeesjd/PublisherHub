# Tour Brief

## Tour title
Cinque Terre Full-Day Tour from Milan: Riomaggiore, Manarola & Monterosso

## Brand
Milano Adventures

## Product reference code
187808P109 _(also seen as `187808P82` in Viator URL and `108` as internal code — flagged for human review in source-facts.md)_

## Core offer
A shared full-day guided tour from Milan to Cinque Terre with travel by GT coach or minivan, Cinque Terre Card local train access, free time in Riomaggiore, Manarola, and Monterosso, an optional seasonal panoramic ferry ride, and a limoncino or limoncello tasting.

## Traveler intent
This product targets travelers based in Milan who are already considering a day trip to Cinque Terre and want a guided, organized way to experience the coast without arranging coach, train, and timing logistics on their own.

## Funnel stage
Primarily BOFU, with strong MOFU support for travelers comparing guided vs self-planned visits.

## Conversion goal
Drive direct bookings on the website first using `{{WebsiteLink}}`, while using the verified TripAdvisor and Viator listings only as secondary trust or alternate discovery references.

## Positioning
Position this experience as a convenient, fixed-departure full-day Cinque Terre day trip from Milan for travelers who want scenic coastal villages, guided support, and smoother logistics in a single booking.

## What makes this product compelling
- Departures from Milan meeting point at Piazza IV Novembre in front of the Hotel Gallia bus stop
- Fixed weekly departures on Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday at 07:00
- Full-day format with approximately 13 hours 30 minutes total duration
- Air-conditioned GT coach travel toward the Ligurian coast
- Guided shared experience with an expert tour leader
- Cinque Terre Card included for local train access between villages
- Free time in Riomaggiore, Manarola, and Monterosso
- Limoncino or limoncello tasting included
- Access to Cinque Terre National Park
- Instant confirmation, paper or mobile ticket accepted
- English and Spanish language support across in-person, audio, and written formats

## Audience fit
Best for:
- visitors staying in Milan who want a long but organized coastal day trip
- travelers who prefer guided logistics instead of self-planning
- couples, friends, and small groups looking for a scenic shared tour
- travelers comfortable with a full-day schedule and moderate walking
- families with children aged 4 and up (children priced separately, infants 0–3 sit on laps at no cost)

Not ideal for:
- travelers needing wheelchair accessibility
- travelers seeking hotel pickup and drop-off
- travelers wanting a private experience
- travelers booking on Tue, Thu, Fri, or Sun (no departure on those days)

## Content angle
Focus on ease, scenery, and confidence in booking. Emphasize that this is a practical, time-efficient way to visit Cinque Terre from Milan with all major transport coordination included. Anchor mid-funnel readers on the value of fixed weekly departures and a single guided itinerary.

## Key operational facts to preserve
- Meeting point only; no custom hotel pickup
- Meeting point: Piazza IV Novembre, in front of the Hotel Gallia bus stop, staff in fuchsia shirts, arrive 15 minutes early
- End point is the same as start point
- Shared group experience, max 22 travelers
- Departure days: Mon, Wed, Sat at 07:00
- Ferry ride is seasonal and depends on weather and sea conditions
- Itinerary may vary in bad weather
- Comfortable shoes are suggested
- Most travelers can participate
- Infants must sit on laps
