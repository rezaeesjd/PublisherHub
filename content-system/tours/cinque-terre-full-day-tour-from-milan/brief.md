# Content Brief

## Objective
Drive direct, high-intent bookings for a full-day Cinque Terre tour from Milan while keeping OTA links as supporting trust channels.

## Funnel strategy
- **BOFU (primary):** booking-ready readers searching day-trip/tour queries.
- **MOFU:** travelers comparing transport convenience and logistics from Milan.
- **TOFU:** destination-curious readers researching Cinque Terre villages.

## Posting frequency
- 1 BOFU conversion page (this package) + 2 support posts/month (comparison + planning guides).

## Distribution approach
- Use blog post as a landing-blog hybrid.
- Internal links from related Milan day-trip content.
- Preserve Viator/TripAdvisor as secondary trust signals.
