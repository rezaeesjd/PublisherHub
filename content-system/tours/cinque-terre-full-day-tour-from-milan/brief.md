# Content Brief

## Objective
Create a MOFU landing-blog hybrid that helps travelers comparing day trip options from Milan decide to book this Cinque Terre tour.

## Funnel role
- Stage: MOFU (evaluation/comparison)
- Intent: “Is this the right Cinque Terre day trip for me?”

## Strategy snapshot
- Publish 1 MOFU post now, then 2 supporting TOFU posts and 1 BOFU booking page variant.
- Prioritize long-tail booking-intent keywords first due to stronger conversion potential.
- Use Viator/TripAdvisor social proof as trust aids while pushing direct brand trust through Milano Adventures voice.
