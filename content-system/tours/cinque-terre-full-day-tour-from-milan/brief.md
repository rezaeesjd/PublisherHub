# Content Brief

## Objective
Capture high-intent travelers searching for a one-day Cinque Terre trip from Milan and convert them with clear logistics, realistic expectations, and strong booking CTA.

## Funnel strategy
- **BOFU:** Main landing-blog for immediate booking intent.
- **MOFU:** Comparison follow-up content (guided tour vs DIY by train).
- **TOFU:** Informational content around best time to visit and village highlights.

## Posting frequency recommendation
- Publish 1 BOFU page now.
- Add 2 supporting MOFU/TOFU posts over next 3 weeks.
- Refresh pricing/logistics monthly during peak season.

## Conversion angle
Emphasize convenience: transport included, expert leader, efficient itinerary from Milan, and reduced planning friction.
