# FAQ

## Is this a private tour?
No. This is a shared group tour with a maximum of 22 travelers.

## Where does the tour start and end?
The meeting point is Piazza IV Novembre, 20124 Milano MI, Italy, in front of the Hotel Gallia bus stop near Milano Centrale station. The tour ends at the same point.

## How do I find the guide at the meeting point?
The team wears fuchsia shirts at the Hotel Gallia bus stop on Piazza IV Novembre. Plan to arrive about 15 minutes before the start time so check-in is unhurried.

## What time does the tour start?
The tour starts at 07:00.

## Which days does the tour run?
Departures are scheduled on Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Hotel pickup and drop-off are not included.

## Which Cinque Terre villages are included in the itinerary?
The product setup includes free time in Riomaggiore, Manarola, and Monterosso.

## How long is the tour?
The total duration is approximately 13 hours and 30 minutes.

## What transport is included?
The product setup includes travel by air-conditioned GT coach or minivan from Milan to Cinque Terre and a day pass for local trains through the Cinque Terre Card.

## Is the ferry ride always included?
Not always. The panoramic ferry ride is seasonal and depends on sea and weather conditions. On some days, especially outside peak season, it may not operate and the itinerary may vary.

## What is included in the tour?
Included items listed in the product setup are:
- expert tour leader
- landing and facility fees
- limoncino or limoncello tasting
- travel by GT coach or minivan from Milan to Cinque Terre
- free time in Riomaggiore, Manarola, and Monterosso
- day pass for local trains through the Cinque Terre Card
- access to Cinque Terre National Park

## Is this tour suitable for everyone?
Most travelers can participate. The difficulty level is rated easy, but it is a long day with walking between train stations and through village streets. The tour is not wheelchair accessible. Comfortable shoes are suggested.

## Are children and infants allowed?
Yes. Adults are priced for ages 12–99, children for ages 4–11, and infants for ages 0–3. Infants must sit on laps. Current pricing is shown on the booking page.

## What languages are available?
The product setup lists English and Spanish for in-person, audio, and written formats.

## How do I receive my ticket?
Confirmation is instant after booking, and either a paper or mobile ticket is accepted. One ticket per booking, and entry is direct.

## Is there a cancellation policy?
The product setup lists a cancellation window relative to the start time. Please confirm the exact cancellation cutoff on the official booking page before booking.
