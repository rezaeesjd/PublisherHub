# FAQ
## How long is the tour?
The experience lasts about 13 hours and 30 minutes in total.

## Where is the meeting point in Milan?
At Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia (near Milano Centrale station).

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet the team directly at the departure point.

## Which villages are visited?
The tour covers Cinque Terre villages including Riomaggiore, Manarola, Corniglia, Vernazza, and Monterosso.

## Is the ferry always included?
The panoramic ferry segment is seasonal and depends on sea/weather conditions.
