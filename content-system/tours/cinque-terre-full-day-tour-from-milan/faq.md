# FAQ

## How long is this Cinque Terre day tour from Milan?
The tour runs approximately 13 hours and 30 minutes.

## Where is the meeting point in Milan?
At Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia near Milano Centrale.

## Is ferry transport always included?
The panoramic ferry ride is seasonal and depends on sea/weather conditions.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Hotel pickup and drop-off are excluded.
