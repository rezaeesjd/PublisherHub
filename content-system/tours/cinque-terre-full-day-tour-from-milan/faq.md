# FAQ – Cinque Terre Full-Day Tour from Milan

## 1) Where does the tour start and end?
The tour departs from and returns to Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia bus stop in the Milano Centrale area. Your guide will be wearing a fuchsia shirt. Arrive at least 15 minutes before the tour starts.

## 2) What time does the tour depart?
Departure is at 07:00.

## 3) Which days does the tour run?
The tour departs on Monday, Wednesday, and Saturday.

## 4) How long is the full tour?
The experience lasts 13 hours and 30 minutes, including roundtrip travel time from Milan and all stops in the five villages.

## 5) Which Cinque Terre villages are visited?
The tour covers all five villages: Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, Corniglia, Manarola, and Riomaggiore.

## 6) Is train transport between the villages included?
Yes. Train tickets between Cinque Terre villages are included in the price.

## 7) Is the ferry ride included?
A panoramic ferry ride is part of the itinerary and is included in the price. However, it is a seasonal activity and subject to sea and weather conditions. On certain days — especially outside peak season — the ferry may not operate for safety or logistical reasons. The rest of the tour continues as planned regardless.

## 8) What languages does the guide speak?
Your expert tour leader provides live commentary in English and Spanish.

## 9) How much does the tour cost?
Adult tickets (ages 12–99) are €275 per person. Children (ages 4–11) are €157. Infants (ages 0–3) join for free. Pricing is valid from May 1, 2026.

## 10) Is there a minimum group size?
Yes. The tour operates with a minimum of 15 travelers.

## 11) What is the maximum group size?
The maximum group size is 22 travelers.

## 12) How difficult is the tour physically?
The tour is rated Easy and is suitable for most fitness levels.

## 13) What information do I need to provide at booking?
You will need the lead traveler's name and phone number, plus the full names and dates of birth of all travelers in your group.

## 14) What ticket format is accepted?
Both paper tickets and mobile tickets are accepted. One ticket covers the entire booking.
