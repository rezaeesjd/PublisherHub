# FAQ

## How long is this tour?
The experience is listed as approximately 13 hours and 30 minutes.

## Where is the meeting point in Milan?
At Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia near Milano Centrale.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet at the designated meeting point.

## Is the ferry always included?
The panoramic ferry segment is seasonal and depends on sea/weather conditions.

## Which languages are listed?
English and Spanish are listed in the source details.
