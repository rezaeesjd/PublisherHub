# FAQ

## How long is the Cinque Terre full-day tour from Milan?
The tour duration is listed as approximately 13 hours and 30 minutes.

## Where do we meet in Milan?
The listed meeting point is Piazza IV Novembre, 20124 Milano MI, Italy.

## What time does the tour start?
The listed start time is 07:00.

## Is this a small group or a large bus tour?
The source details list a traveler cap of up to 22 participants.

## Is the ferry always included?
Ferry routing is seasonal and can change based on sea/weather conditions.

## Can I book directly on the operator website?
Yes. A direct Milano Adventures booking link is available as the primary CTA.

## Are alternate booking platforms available?
Yes. Viator and TripAdvisor listing links are included as secondary options.
