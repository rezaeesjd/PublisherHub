# FAQ

## Is this a full-day tour?
Yes. The listed total duration is about 13 hours and 30 minutes.

## Where does the tour start in Milan?
At Piazza IV Novembre, in front of Hotel Gallia at the designated bus stop.

## Which languages are available?
English and Spanish.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet at the stated meeting point.

## Is the ferry ride always included?
The panoramic ferry section is seasonal and depends on sea/weather conditions.
