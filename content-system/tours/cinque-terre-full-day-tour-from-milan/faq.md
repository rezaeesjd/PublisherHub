# FAQ

## Is this a full-day trip from Milan?
Yes. The experience runs approximately 13 hours and 30 minutes including transfer time.

## Which Cinque Terre villages are covered?
Riomaggiore, Manarola, Monterosso al Mare, Vernazza, and Corniglia.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet at Piazza IV Novembre near Milano Centrale.

## Is the ferry always included?
No. The ferry segment is seasonal and depends on sea/weather conditions.

## What languages are available?
English and Spanish.
