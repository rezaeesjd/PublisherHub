# FAQ

## How long is the class?
Approximately 3 hours, including the lesson, tasting, and the meal at the end.

## Where is the meeting point in Milan?
The Info Point inside Mercato Centrale Milan, at Via Giovanni Battista Sammartini, 1 — at the corner with Piazza IV Novembre, next to Milano Centrale rail station.

## How many people are in the group?
The format is strict small-group: up to 8 participants.

## Is dinner included?
Yes — dinner based on what you've prepared, with drinks included.

## Are vegetarian and allergy needs covered?
The class is suitable for vegetarians on request. Please inform the team of any food intolerance or allergy in advance.

## Is hotel pickup included?
No. Travelers meet at the Mercato Centrale info point.

## Is the venue accessible?
The source notes the venue is not wheelchair accessible and not stroller accessible. The team asks travelers to inform them in advance of any special needs or impaired mobility so they can do their best to accommodate.
