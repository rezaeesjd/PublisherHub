# Pizza and Gelato Cooking Class in Milan: Small-Group, Hands-On

If you want a culinary experience in Milan that goes beyond a restaurant table, this small-group class with **Milano Adventures** lets you make pizza and gelato yourself, then sit down and eat what you've cooked.

## Why travelers choose this class
- Strict small-group format — up to 8 participants
- Hands-on pizza and gelato preparation with a local chef
- Tuscan wine and olive oil tasting (soft drinks for children)
- Dinner based on what you prepared, with drinks included
- Certificate of Attendance and a Recipe Booklet to take home

## What to expect
You'll meet at the Info Point inside Mercato Centrale Milan, on Via Giovanni Battista Sammartini 1 — right next to Milano Centrale station. The session runs for around 3 hours, including the cooking lesson, the tasting, and the meal at the end.

The class is suitable for vegetarians on request, and the team asks travelers to flag any food intolerances or allergies in advance.

## Is this class right for you?
A good fit for couples, families with older children, and food enthusiasts who want a relaxed, social evening rather than a sightseeing day. Hotel pickup is not included; travelers meet at the Mercato Centrale info point.
